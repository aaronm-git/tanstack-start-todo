# Authentication

Authentication lets you create an account, sign in, and access the app.

## What you can do

- Create an account with email + password
- Sign in and access authenticated parts of the app
- Sign out to end your session
- Reset your password if you forget it

## How to use it

- **Sign up**: Go to the login page and choose “Create an account”.
- **Sign in**: Enter your email + password and submit.
- **Forgot password**: Use the “Forgot password?” link on the login page.
